Pre-check your risks of infection before visiting public areas and public transport or even travelling to other countries. It will help your clients to plan their trips and travels beforehand and learn all the safe options in advance.

Tracking the risk of being infected by COVID-19 relies on facts and records from proven scientific research studies, data from WHO and authoritative universities, monitoring your anonymous movements and the public areas you visited.
