About Us

We add value to your ideas and turn them into a working business with a solid IT background

Cortex Technology is a custom, turnkey, technology development and investment company with its roots in Krasnodar, Russia.

We started as a web development company in the early 1990s, providing services to small and medium size enterprises and progressed to multinationals and government contracts involving process automation, multilevel reporting, corporate accounting software and interactive touch systems.

Today, we deliver institutional grade solutions and development services to leading companies and private investors across many different markets and industries.
